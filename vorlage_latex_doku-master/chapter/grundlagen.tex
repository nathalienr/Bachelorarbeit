\chapter{Fundamentals}
\label{cha:fundamentals}

Targeted theoretical fundamentals, both domain-specific and methodological.

The fundamentals include, for example, details regarding the problem statement, the state of the art, and other essential prerequisites required for the concept development, implementation, and verification.

Fundamentals always relate to the subsequent chapters. This connection should occasionally be made explicit so that it becomes clear within this chapter where and for what purpose these basics are needed and applied.
